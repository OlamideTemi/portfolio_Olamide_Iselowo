Nigerian Graduates Survey

Accordingly to March 16, 2021 publication of Premium Times, a renowned Nigerian newspaper, Nigeria's unemployment rate rose to 33.3% to become the second highest in the world. This project's analyses studies the employment status, income level and proportions on various sectors of graduates over the years. Self employment and post graduate studies are also factors; and the analyses ends with visuals of those who did not complete youth service, one of the requirements for employment in Nigeria.
